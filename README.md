# AI Execution Lab

Applied AI modules translating business strategy into working technical solutions.

This repository serves as a structured execution layer complementing my strategy portfolio, focusing on building hands-on, business-oriented AI use cases.

## Approach

Each module represents an independent applied case developed through:

- Data exploration and processing
- Machine learning or analytical modeling
- Translation of outputs into business insights

The goal is not technical experimentation alone, but the practical application of AI to support decision-making and operational execution.

## Focus Areas

- Forecasting & planning
- Decision intelligence
- AI-driven business applications

## Modules

- 01 — Retail Demand Forecasting *(in progress)*
- 02 — Customer Segmentation *(planned)*
- 03 — GenAI Reporting *(planned)*

## Objective

Bridge the gap between AI strategy and execution by developing tangible, business-aligned AI use cases.

Each module is designed to demonstrate how AI concepts can be translated into real-world applications with measurable impact.
